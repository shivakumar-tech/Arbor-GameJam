Frequently Asked Questions

What is Designers Odyssey?

Designers Odyssey is a design competition organized by Arbor International School, where students can showcase their creativity and design skills in various categories, including 3D Modeling, Product Design, Graphic Design, and Game Development.

How can I register for the competition?

You can register for the competition by visiting the registration page on our website. Fill out the form with your details and submit your entry.

What are the judging criteria?

The judging criteria vary by category but typically include factors such as adherence to theme, creativity, originality, attention to detail, and overall presentation of the work.

Can I participate in multiple categories?

Yes, you are allowed to participate in multiple categories, provided you meet the eligibility requirements for each category.

When is the deadline for submissions?

The deadline for submissions will be communicated on the registration page and via email after you register. Make sure to submit your designs before the specified deadline to be eligible for judging.
